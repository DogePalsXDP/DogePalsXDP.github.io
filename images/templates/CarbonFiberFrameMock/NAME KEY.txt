The following helps explain the naming convention for the Frames:

**Mock names are for templates with "fake" transparency for the image to look good, non Mock folders will be actual useable templates.**

COLOR - FRAME MATERIAL/LOOK - CORNER SHAPE - ART SIZE (UPPER AREA) - LOGO LOCATION

EXAMPLE: PinkCFBevel680x800TL
Pink - Carbon Fiber - Bevel - 680 x 800 - Top Left

LOGO AREA KEY
TL = Top Left
LR = Lower Right
BL = Bottom Left
BM = Bottom Middle
